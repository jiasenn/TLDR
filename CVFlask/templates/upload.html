Term 7 CV: Ocular Disease Recognition

Singapore is an aging society where ocular diseases such as diabetic retinopathy, glaucoma and age-related macular degeneration are becoming more commonplace.

As such, our group has designed a computer vision model to provide predictions on the possibility of the aforementioned conditions in order to propose the digitization and the streamlining of eye diagnosis processes, hence making it more convenient for people to screen their eye health.
